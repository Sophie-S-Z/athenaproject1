About the Show

The Queen's Gambit is a critically acclaimed Netflix miniseries that follows the life of chess prodigy Beth Harmon as she rises through the ranks of the chess world while battling personal struggles. Set during the Cold War era, the show beautifully blends drama, strategy, and character development.

Fun Facts

  • The chess positions in the show are based on real grandmaster games, some of which are famous in chess history.
  • Beth Harmon's character was inspired by real-life chess prodigies like Bobby Fischer and Judit PolgÃ¡r.
  • The green pills Beth takes are based on Librium, a tranquilizer used in the 1960s.
  • After the show's release, sales of chess sets increased by over 1000% in some countries.
  • Many of the costumes and sets use subtle chess motifs, such as checkerboard patterns and queen crowns.
  • The series won 11 Primetime Emmy Awards, including Outstanding Limited Series.
