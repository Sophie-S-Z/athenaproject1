About the Show

The Queen's Gambit is a critically acclaimed Netflix miniseries that follows the life of chess prodigy Beth Harmon as she rises through the ranks of the chess world while battling personal struggles. Set during the Cold War era, the show beautifully blends drama, strategy, and character development.

Main Characters

  • Beth Harmon – The chess prodigy and protagonist.
  • Alma Wheatley – Beth's adoptive mother.
  • Mr. Shaibel – The janitor who teaches Beth chess.
  • Benny Watts – A chess champion and Beth's friend.
  • Harry Beltik – A chess player and Beth's mentor.
  • Jolene – Beth's childhood friend from the orphanage.

Fun Facts

  • The chess games in the series were choreographed by grandmasters Garry Kasparov and Bruce Pandolfini.
  • The title refers to a popular chess opening called the Queen's Gambit.
  • Anya Taylor-Joy, who plays Beth, learned chess specifically for the role.
  • The show initiated a worldwide chess boom after its release.
